- **Terminal Logging**: Button clicks are logged to the terminal window

- **Cursor Trail**: Custom circular cursor that follows mouse movement
- **Pulse on Click**: Cursor scales up and brightens when clicking
- **Smooth Transitions**: Cursor movement is smoothly interpolated
